Your helpful advisor for Pokémon Go battles!

Ever fight a Team GO Rocket Leader again and again only to lose each time. Then you check out the type tips in the app, but you don't know which Pokémon are which type? This app is for you!

How to use this site?

Getting Started

To get started, create an account with your email address, then head over to My Pokollection and register the Pokémon that you have in you Pokémon Go collection.

Getting Recommendations

After you have registered Pokémon in your collection, click on Get Recomendations and put in the type or the specific Pokémon used by your opponent. Then, just click the submit button to get recommendations based on your collection and favorites within the collection.
